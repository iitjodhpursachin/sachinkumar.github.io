About the Archive

This digital archive preserves Chhath Puja folk songs from Bihar. It documents song recordings, interviews with participants, and photographs of ritual spaces. The archive includes transcription and translation of lyrics and preserves songs of early singers such as Kumud Akhauri, Kali Devi, and Vindhyavasini Devi.

Important Singers
