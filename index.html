About the Archive

This digital archive preserves Chhath Puja folk songs from Bihar. It documents recordings, interviews with participants, photographs of ritual spaces, and includes transcription and translation of song lyrics. The archive also preserves songs of early singers such as Kumud Akhauri, Kali Devi, and Vindhyavasini Devi.
